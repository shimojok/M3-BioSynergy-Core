## 2. Scientific Basis

### 2.1 Soil Microbiome Restoration
Coffee soils are often degraded by:
- Acidification
- Nutrient depletion
- Fungal pathogens

MBT55 restores:
- Microbial diversity
- Enzyme activity
- Carbon–nitrogen balance

### 2.2 Flavor Precursor Enhancement
Enzyme cascades increase:
- Amino acids
- Sugars
- Volatile precursors

Leading to:
- Improved aroma
- Enhanced sweetness
- Reduced bitterness
